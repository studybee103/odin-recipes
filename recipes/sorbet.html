Pineapple Sorbet

Sweet and tangy pineapple sorbet to refresh your summer

If it ever gets too hot where you live, pineapple sorbet is just the right thing for you!

Ingredients

  • 1 pineapple - peeled, cored, and cut into chunks
  • 1 (6 ounce) can pineapple juice
  • Â¾ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon vodka
  • 2 leaves fresh mint
  1. Place pineapple, pineapple juice, sugar, lime juice, vodka, and mint in a food processor and process until smooth.
  2. Pour mixture into an ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturer's instructions, about 20 minutes. Transfer to an airtight container; put in the freezer for at least 6 hours before serving so it can firm up.
